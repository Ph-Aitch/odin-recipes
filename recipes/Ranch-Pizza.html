Chicken, Bacon, and Ranch Pizza



Description

Ranch and bacon lovers will love everything about this pizza. It's easy to make using shortcuts like refrigerated canned pizza crust and pre-cooked chicken and bacon.


Ingredients

  • 1 (10 ounce) container refrigerated pizza crust

  • 1 cup sour cream

  • 2 tablespoons ranch seasoning mix

  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided

  • 1 Â½ cups cubed, cooked chicken

  • 1 medium tomato, diced

  • 6 strips cooked bacon, chopped

  • 3 thin slices red onion, separated into rings


Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease a baking sheet.

  2. Spread pizza crust onto the prepared baking sheet.

  3. Bake in the preheated oven until edges just start to brown, 7 to 8 minutes.

  4. Combine sour cream and ranch mix in a bowl; spread onto warm pizza crust. Sprinkle with 1/2 mozzarella cheese. Top with chicken, tomato, bacon, and red onion. Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 mozzarella cheese.

  5. Bake in the preheated oven until crust is browned to your liking and cheese is melted, 6 to 10 minutes more.
